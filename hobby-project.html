BONSAI

The craft of cultivating and training miniature trees in pots, originated in East Asia.

In the ancient discipline of Feng Shui, bonsai have long been revered for their capacity to attract life energy into a space and generously distribute them to everyone who enters. When a bonsai is the center of attention, conversation, and living forces, it may instantly provide happiness and contentment to everyone who sees it.
